Pozole

Description

Delicius mexican soup make with the tears and blood of your enemies, with all the flavor of the pain and missfourtune of others

Highly recommended to eat with your family and friends. Remember sharing is caring.

Ingredients:

  • Mexican acne
  • Some type of meet
  • Alot of blood
  • Red cucumber
  • Shrek layers
  • Yummy leafs
  • Flavor crystals
  • Wotar

Steps

  1. Put the mexican acne in a pot with boiling wotar
  2. When the acne explodes put the meet
  3. Add some flavor crystals
  4. Add some yummy leafs
  5. Add blood of your enemies
  6. Let it cook for about a eternity
  7. Cut the red cucumber and de shrek layers for garnish
  8. When the pot start screaming cut the fire and server with the garnish
  9. Enjoy
